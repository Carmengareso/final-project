ABOUT ME

Since discovering the world of design, I have been fascinated by its ability to communicate, excite, and transform ideas into visual experiences. I don't limit myself to a single specialty, as I enjoy exploring different areas. For me, each project is an opportunity to experiment, learn, and find new forms of expression.

My style is characterized by cleanliness and balance, without reaching extreme minimalism. I like my designs to be visually appealing, but also functional and well-structured. I am fluent in various Adobe tools as well as Procreate and Figma, which allows me to adapt to different types of projects.

I am a curious person who is constantly learning. I am motivated by new challenges and discovering trends, techniques, and technologies that help me continue to evolve as a designer.
